Contract of Carriage

This Contract of Carriage ("Contract") is entered into between GuaranteedFlights.com, hereinafter referred to as the "Provider," and the individual or entity making a flight booking through the Provider's website, hereinafter referred to as the "Passenger." Once a ticket is purchased the selected Airline Carrier’s Contract of Carriage is also entered into between the Passenger and that Airline.

Guarantee of Flights

  • The Provider guarantees that all flights booked through its website, including those operated by Participating Airlines, will be provided to the Passenger as per the confirmed itinerary.
  • In the event of flight cancellation, for reasons attributable to the Provider or Participating Airline, the Provider will simply provide a refund or reallocation of original funds toward an alternate flight, rental car or hotel. The Passenger should also contact the airline to take advantage of any reaccommodating or refund they may provide as well.

Ticketing and Booking

  • The Passenger agrees to provide accurate and complete information at the time of booking, including personal details, contact information, and travel preferences.
  • The Provider reserves the right to refuse or cancel bookings that do not comply with its policies or applicable regulations.

Payment and Refunds

  • The Passenger agrees to pay the full amount due for the booked flights at the time of booking, unless otherwise specified.
  • In the event of flight cancellation, the Provider will facilitate refunds in accordance with applicable regulations and its refund policy. Unless the Passenger chooses to utilize said funds toward an alternate flight, car rental or hotel room. Note: Please see (Section 2.4) in the Terms and Conditions regarding alternate flight accommodation bookings.

Passenger Responsibilities

  • The Passenger is responsible for reviewing and complying with their selected Airline’s travel requirements, including but not limited to visa, passport, and health regulations.
  • The Passenger agrees to abide by the rules and regulations of the airlines operating their booked flights, including check-in and baggage policies.

Changes and Cancellations

  • Within 24 hours of booking a ticket the Passenger may request changes or cancellations to their bookings in accordance with The Provider and their Selected Airline Carrier’s policies and applicable regulations. Outside of 24 hours the Passenger should contact the Airline directly.
  • The Provider and its Airline Carriers have the right to levy fees or charges for changes or cancellations made by the Passenger, as outlined in its policies.

Liability

  • The Provider shall not be liable for any loss, damage, injury, or inconvenience arising from circumstances beyond its control, including but not limited to acts of nature, political unrest, or mechanical failures.
  • The Provider's liability for any claims arising from the provision of flight services shall be limited to the amount paid by the Passenger for the affected booking.

Governing Law

  • This Contract shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Florida, without regard to its conflict of law provisions.

Entire Agreement

  • This Contract constitutes the entire agreement between the Provider and the Passenger concerning the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior or contemporaneous agreements and understandings, whether written or oral.

Amendments

  • No modification or amendment to this Contract shall be valid unless made in writing and signed by both parties.

Severability

  • If any provision of this Contract is held to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.
