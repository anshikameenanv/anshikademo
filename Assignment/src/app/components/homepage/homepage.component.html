Flight Booking FAQs

Can I book one way flight tickets on GuaranteedFlights.com?

Yes

How do I find the cheapest flights on GuaranteedFlights.com?

Our ticket search engine can be sorted by ticket price. Simply choose "Low to High" and the cheapest flights will start at the top of the page.

Does GuaranteedFlights.com charge credit card fees?

No

Am I really guaranteed a refund or credit toward a rebooked flight if my original flight is cancelled?

100% guaranteed. You can even take advantage of any flight credit or accommodation the airline offers you as well.

What if I encounter booking issues?

If you encounter any issues while booking your flight on GuaranteedFlights.com, you can contact our customer support team for assistance via email.

 Special assistance requests on GuaranteedFlights.com?
Yes, GuaranteedFlights.com allows you to request special assistance or accommodations, such as wheelchair assistance or dietary preferences, during the booking process.
How can I manage my flight booking on GuaranteedFlights.com?

You can manage your flight booking by logging into your GuaranteedFlights.com account and accessing the "My Bookings" section, where you can view, modify, or cancel your reservations.

 Changing flight itinerary on GuaranteedFlights.com?

If you need to make changes to your flight itinerary, such as date or time changes, you can contact GuaranteedFlights.com's customer support team for assistance. Please note that fees and restrictions may apply.

Baggage restrictions on GuaranteedFlights.com flights?

Baggage allowances vary by airline and fare type. GuaranteedFlights.com provides information on baggage policies during the booking process, and you can also check directly with the airline for specific details.
How far in advance can I book a flight?

As far in advance as the airline you are booking with allows.

How do I collect my refund if my flight is cancelled?

When you receive your cancelation authorization you will be directed to the redemption page. You will then have the choice to choose a refund or to rebook your flight with your original puchased ticket price credited toward the new ticket. If you choose refund, follow the prompts and recieve your refund.

Is it safe to book flights through GuaranteedFlights.com ?

Yes, GuaranteedFlights.com prioritizes the security and privacy of our customers' information. We use industry-standard encryption technology to protect your personal and payment details.

 What is a Flexible Ticket?

Flight prices can fluctuate based on various factors, including demand, seat availability, and airline pricing strategies. While it's possible for prices to decrease closer to the departure date, particularly if there is unsold inventory, this isn't always the case, especially for popular routes and peak travel times. Generally, booking in advance is advisable to secure the best fares, but monitoring prices and being flexible with travel dates can also help you find deals closer to departure.

Does GuaranteedFlights.com offer travel insurance for flight bookings?

GuaranteedFlights.com does not offer travel insurance for flight bookings.

 Seat preference selection on GuaranteedFlights.com ?

Yes, GuaranteedFlights.com allows you to choose your seat preference during the booking process, subject to availability.

 Does GuaranteedFlights.com offer last-minute flight deals

GuaranteedFlights.com may offer last-minute flight deals based on availability and demand. Check our website regularly or sign up for our newsletter to stay updated on the latest offers.

 Unaccompanied minors bookings on GuaranteedFlights.com ?

Yes, GuaranteedFlights.com offers the option to book flights for unaccompanied minors, subject to the airline's policies and age restrictions. Additional fees may apply.
